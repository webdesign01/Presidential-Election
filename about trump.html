About Trump

Full Name Date of Birth Hometown Political Party Spouse Children Pets
Donald John Trump, Sr. June 14th, 1946 Queen's, New York Republican Party Melania Trump Ivanka Trump, Tiffany Trump, Barron Trump, Eric Trump, Donald Trump Jr. None

Donald Trump grew up in Queen's, New York with his parents and four siblings. After graduating from the University of Pennsylvania, Trump joined the family business, the Trump Organization. Later he acquired a great amount of wealth which he used in various ways. This year, Trump is running for the U.S. Presidency as the Republican Candidate. His main aspiration is to make America great again.

Pros

  • Has many years of business experience running large corporations
  • Adresses gun violence, but supports the right of being armed for defense purposes
  • Fresh face because he doesn't come from a political background
  • He wants to "Make America Great Aagain"
  • Received more delegates in the primaries than any other candidate in the republican party.

Cons

  • Doesn't have a strong government related background
  • Has no filter and says whatever he wants, allowing him to be obnoxious at times
  • Hypocritical because he says he wants to create more jobs by manufacturing in the U.S. but produces his own products overseas
  • Alienates minorities
  • Unpredictable for someone who could be running the most powerful country in the world
